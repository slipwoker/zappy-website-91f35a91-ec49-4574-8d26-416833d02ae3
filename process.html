Frequently Asked Questions

We know custom orders can feel like a big decision. Here are answers to the questions we hear most often—everything from timing and sizes to dietary needs and how we bring your vision to life.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend ordering at least 2–3 weeks ahead for custom cakes and specialty items, especially during busy seasons. For simpler treats like cookies or cupcakes, a week's notice usually works beautifully. Rush orders may be possible—just reach out and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

How do I know what size cake to order?

We're happy to guide you! As a general rule, a 6-inch cake serves 8–12 people, an 8-inch serves 15–20, and a 10-inch serves 25–30. If you're unsure, just let us know your guest count and we'll recommend the perfect size for your celebration.

Can you match specific colors or themes?

Absolutely! We love bringing your vision to life. Share photos, color swatches, or even Pinterest boards with us—we'll work closely with you to match your colors and capture the mood you're imagining. Custom colors and themed decorations are one of our favorite parts of the process.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes, we take allergies and dietary needs seriously. We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free options for many of our items. Because we're a small kitchen, we can't guarantee zero cross-contamination, but we take every precaution. Let us know your needs and we'll discuss what's possible.

Do you deliver, or is it pickup only?

We offer both! Local delivery is available within a 15-mile radius for an additional fee, and we'll coordinate a delivery window that works for you. Pickup is always free—just swing by our kitchen during our scheduled pickup hours and we'll have everything ready and waiting.

What if I have an idea but don't know exactly what I want?

That's completely normal! Share your inspiration—colors, flavors, occasions, or even just a feeling—and we'll sketch out ideas together. We're here to guide you from concept to finished creation, making sure every detail feels right before we start baking.

Can I make changes after I've confirmed my order?

Small tweaks are usually fine if you let us know early enough—flavor swaps, color adjustments, or design refinements. Once we've started baking or decorating (typically 3–5 days before your event), changes become tricky. The sooner you reach out, the more flexible we can be.

How should I store and serve my custom order?

Most cakes and treats should be refrigerated until a few hours before serving—this keeps everything fresh and safe. We'll include specific storage and serving instructions with your order. For best flavor and texture, let cakes sit at room temperature for 30–60 minutes before cutting.
